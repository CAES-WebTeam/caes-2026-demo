CAES 2026 Redesign: Design Demo

Keep in mind all ideas aren't set in stone and page contents are filler! A design system with common elements and components is linked at the bottom of this page.

Pages — templates

8 pages
homepage

CAES Homepage

The composed homepage — all sections stitched together in order.
academics

Academics landing

A top-level section landing — a light page header instead of a full hero, then two parameterized tabbed sections for the menu's two content areas (Explore Academics and Student Life), capped with a closing CTA.
programs

Degrees and Programs directory

Full page — searchable degree list with modal-based filters for Program Type and Department.
program-detail

Program Detail page

Single degree landing page (Horticulture) — black title area with faded photo, sticky scroll-spy nav, alternating section bands, careers + outcome bars, paying-for-college, and advisor contacts.
directory

Our People — directory

Faceted people directory (50 from people.csv) — sidebar filters (Employee Type, Department, Program Area, Campus) with search, collapsible groups, and pagination. Cards fall back to an initials monogram when there's no photo.
experts

Search by Expertise

Expertise-first companion to the directory (same people, same profile links). Top filters (Expertise / Department / Campus) open in modals — the degree-programs pattern — with search across expertise, name, and title. Results are a scannable list of expert rows with their expertise areas trailing.
interior-page

Interior page

An interior page with no section rail (the main nav covers where it sits). Header → intro text + video → full-width on-page zone (news feed) → section footer. Shown as the Food Science and Technology research page.
interior-page-sidebar

Interior page with sidebar

The same interior page, plus a right rail of sibling pages. The rail shows only the direct parent + its children — breadcrumbs handle climbing up. Keeps the full-width on-page zone and section footer too. Generic, self-documenting mockup.
